How to send money online?
We debit the senders Card (debit, credit and prepaid), for some countries we can debit your bank account directly and instantly credit the beneficiary’s bank account in specified country.
What Countries do you provide instant settlement to beneficiary?
For now, we can settle instantly to all Nigerian Bank account, as long as we have successfully debited your card. We can also settle instantly to Ghana, Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda, however, we are yet to open up these corridors.
How long will it take for the beneficiary to get money?
All transfers on Nodtransfer are settled instantly in seconds.
